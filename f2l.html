What's an F2L?

F2L stands for First Two Layers, the second step in the CFOP method after solving the cross,F2L in total have 41 basic algorithms . Instead of placing corners and edges separately like in the beginner method, F2L pairs each white corner with its matching edge and inserts them together into the correct slot. There are four of these pairs to solve. This method is faster and more efficient because it reduces the number of moves. F2L can be learned intuitively by understanding how pieces move and interact, or more advanced cubers can memorize specific cases to solve pairs even quicker. While tricky at first, F2L becomes easier with practice and is essential for improving speed.
