PLL

PLL (Permutation of the Last Layer) is the final step in the CFOP method for solving a 3x3 Rubik’s Cube. It focuses on rearranging (permuting) the last layer’s corners and edges while keeping their orientation fixed. There are 21 different PLL algorithms, each designed to solve a specific pattern of last-layer permutation.
